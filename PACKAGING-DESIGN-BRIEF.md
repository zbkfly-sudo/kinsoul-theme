# Kinsoul Energy — Packaging Design Brief

## Brand Overview

**Brand:** Kinsoul Energy
**Product:** Handcrafted natural pearl & gemstone bracelets
**Price Range:** $139–$365 USD
**Target Customer:** Women aged 28–35, drawn to quiet luxury, natural beauty, and meaningful jewelry
**Brand Philosophy:** "Natural over perfect" — celebrating the organic beauty of genuine materials
**Brand Colors:** Warm ivory `#F9F6F1`, accent brown `#7B6B5D`, deep charcoal `#2C2C2C`

---

## Design Objective

Create a unified packaging system that delivers a premium unboxing experience across all purchase scenarios — whether the customer is buying for herself, her partner, a friend, or family. The packaging should feel like discovering something precious from the earth: warm, natural, but unmistakably refined.

**Key Principle:** One packaging system, executed to perfection. No tiers, no variations. Every customer receives the same experience regardless of price point.

---

## Packaging Architecture: The Layered Reveal

The unboxing follows a deliberate narrative sequence — story first, then beauty, then care.

### Complete Unboxing Journey

```
Outer Mailer (shipping protection)
  → Tissue paper wrapping (tactile transition)
    → Gift box with brand seal
      → [Layer 0 — optional] Gift message card
      → [Layer 1 — Story] Gemstone meaning card
        → [Layer 2 — Reveal] Bracelet on dark velvet bed
          → [Layer 3 — Care] Care guide in lid pocket
            → Cotton storage pouch (alongside box)
```

---

## Component Specifications

### 1. Gift Box (Core)

**Structure:** Two-piece lid-and-base (天地盖), not hinged. The lid lifts off with gentle air resistance, creating a slow 2-second reveal.

**Dimensions:** 10cm × 10cm × 4.5cm (exterior). Interior sized for bracelet to rest in a natural circle.

**Exterior Material:** Rigid board (2mm greyboard) wrapped in natural cotton-linen fabric.

**Exterior Color:** Warm ivory, close to unbleached cotton — matching brand color `#F9F6F1`. The fabric should have visible texture (subtle woven grain), not smooth coated paper.

**Logo Treatment:** Centered on lid top. **Debossed (blind press / letterpress)** — no foil, no ink. The logo is felt by touch, seen by shadow. This is intentional: the brand identity is discovered through texture, not announced through shine.

- Logo text: "KINSOUL ENERGY" in spaced uppercase
- Deboss depth: approximately 0.5mm
- Size: approximately 5cm wide

**Edge Finishing:** Clean, no exposed board edges. Fabric wraps neatly around all edges with mitered corners.

**Magnetic Closure:** Not required. The two-piece structure provides sufficient ceremony. The air-cushion effect of lifting the lid IS the mechanism.

---

### 2. Interior

**Lining Material:** Microsuede (ultrasuede / faux suede) — matte, warm to touch. NOT shiny satin or silk. The texture should feel like touching a smooth river stone.

**Lining Color:** Deep warm grey-brown `#4A4A4A`, matching the brand's body text color. This creates a deliberate contrast when the light lid is lifted — a visual "moment" as the warm exterior gives way to the deeper interior.

**Bracelet Rest:** A molded insert with a circular channel (approximately 7cm diameter ring). The bracelet sits naturally in its worn shape — a circle, not stretched flat. The center of the ring is gently domed (approximately 5mm rise), so the bracelet appears to be draped over a soft hill.

**Insert Construction:** Molded foam or pulp base, covered in the same microsuede. Should feel solid but yielding — not hollow, not hard.

---

### 3. Gemstone Meaning Card (The Emotional Centerpiece)

**This is the most important element.** It sits directly on top of the bracelet, visible immediately when the lid is removed. It is the first thing the customer reads before seeing the jewelry.

**Dimensions:** 9cm × 9cm (fits snugly inside the box, resting on top of the insert)

**Paper Stock:** Uncoated cotton paper, 350gsm minimum. Natural white (not bright white). Should feel thick, soft, and substantial — like handmade paper.

**Edge Treatment:** Deckle edge (torn/feathered) on all four sides. NOT die-cut straight. The irregular edges reinforce the "natural over perfect" brand ethos. This is a signature detail.

**Front (Stone Story):**
- Stone name in serif font (Cormorant Garamond or similar), centered, large
- One poetic line about the stone's meaning below
- Minimal — maximum 15 words total on the front

**Back (Details):**
- 2–3 lines about the stone's origin, properties, and traditional significance
- Set in small sans-serif (Jost or similar), left-aligned
- Brand mark "KINSOUL ENERGY" at bottom in small caps

**Printing:** Single-color letterpress in warm brown/charcoal `#4A4A4A` on natural paper. The slight impression of letterpress adds tactile quality.

**Quantity:** 6 versions required (one per product — see Stone Meaning Card Copy section below). Each product ships with its matching card.

---

### 4. Care Guide Card (6 Versions — Product-Specific)

**Dimensions:** 7cm × 5cm (small, unobtrusive)

**Paper Stock:** Same cotton paper as meaning card, 300gsm

**Quantity:** 6 versions required (one per product, matching the stone meaning card). Each product ships with its matching care card.

**Front Layout:**

Top area — four universal care icons in a row with one-word labels beneath each:

| Icon | Label |
|---|---|
| Water droplet with line through | Water |
| Perfume bottle with line through | Perfume |
| Soft cloth | Wipe |
| Drawstring pouch | Pouch |

Below icons — 2–3 lines of product-specific care text (see copy below). Set in Jost Light, 7pt, charcoal `#4A4A4A`.

**Back:** Centered, two elements only:

- "Last on, first off." in Cormorant Garamond Italic, 10pt
- "KINSOUL ENERGY" in Jost Regular, 6pt, spaced uppercase, below

**Printing:** Single-color (charcoal `#4A4A4A`) on natural white cotton paper. Offset or letterpress.

**Placement:** Tucked into a fabric pocket on the inside of the lid (not loose in the box). The customer discovers it after taking out the bracelet.

### 5. Gift Message Card (Optional — Customer Adds at Checkout)

**When included:** Customer selects "This is a gift" at checkout and enters a personal message (max 150 characters).

**Dimensions:** 8cm × 6cm

**Paper Stock:** Same cotton paper, 300gsm

**Layout:**
- Front: Customer's message printed in a script/handwriting-style font
- Back: Blank, or a subtle "with love, from someone who chose this for you" in small type

**Placement:** Sits on top of the meaning card (Layer 0). If no gift message, this card is omitted.

**Production Note:** This card must be printed per-order. Consider thermal or inkjet printing on pre-cut cotton paper stock. The font should simulate handwriting but remain legible.

---

### 6. Storage Pouch

**Material:** Washed cotton or brushed cotton twill — soft, not stiff. Should feel like a well-worn t-shirt, not like canvas.

**Color:** Warm ivory, matching the box exterior `#F9F6F1`

**Size:** Approximately 10cm × 12cm (large enough for the bracelet to slide in without forcing)

**Closure:** Drawstring with thin leather cord or waxed cotton cord (natural brown tone). NOT satin ribbon.

**Branding:** Brand name "KINSOUL ENERGY" screen-printed or heat-transferred in `#7B6B5D` (warm brown), centered on one side. Small, approximately 3cm wide. Subtle.

**Purpose:** This pouch is the customer's daily companion — used for travel, gym bag, nightstand storage. The brand stays with her long after unboxing.

---

### 7. Outer Shipping Package

**Mailer Type:** Rigid corrugated mailer or tuck-front mailer, sized to fit the gift box plus pouch with minimal void.

**Interior Padding:** Shredded tissue paper or crinkle kraft in ivory/natural tone. NO plastic bubble wrap, NO styrofoam.

**Tissue Wrap:** The gift box is wrapped in one sheet of tissue paper (ivory or pale blush) before being placed in the mailer. This is the first tactile moment — peeling away tissue to reveal the box.

**Seal:** Branded sticker/seal on the tissue paper fold. Circle shape, approximately 3.5cm diameter, featuring the brand logo. This seal is the "threshold" between shipping and experience.

**No Price:** No invoice, packing slip, or price information is included in the physical package. All order documentation is sent via email only.

## Production Notes

### Estimated Unit Cost (RMB)

| Component | Estimated Cost |
|---|---|
| Two-piece box with cotton-linen wrap + deboss | ¥18–25 |
| Microsuede lined insert with ring channel | ¥8–12 |
| Meaning card (letterpress, deckle edge, 6 versions) | ¥2–3 |
| Care card | ¥0.5–1 |
| Gift message card (per-order print) | ¥1–2 |
| Washed cotton pouch with cord + print | ¥5–8 |
| Tissue paper + brand seal sticker | ¥1.5–2 |
| Rigid mailer + crinkle fill | ¥3–5 |
| **Total per unit** | **¥39–58** |

At average order value of $200 (≈¥1,450), packaging cost is approximately **2.7%–4.0%** of revenue.

### Minimum Order Quantities (Typical)
- Boxes: 500–1,000 pcs per run
- Cards: 200–500 pcs per version (letterpress)
- Pouches: 500–1,000 pcs per run
- Brand stickers: 1,000+ pcs per run

### Key Supplier Capabilities Needed
- Rigid box fabrication with fabric wrapping
- Blind debossing / letterpress capability
- Deckle edge cutting or tearing (hand-torn preferred for authenticity)
- Microsuede lining and foam insert molding
- Cotton pouch sewing with drawstring
- Per-order card printing (for gift messages)

## Quality Checklist

Before approving production samples, verify:

- [ ] Lid lifts with gentle resistance (air cushion effect) — not too tight, not too loose
- [ ] Debossed logo is readable by touch with eyes closed
- [ ] Interior color contrast is visible and immediate when lid is removed
- [ ] Meaning card sits flat and covers the bracelet completely
- [ ] Bracelet fits comfortably in the ring channel without forcing
- [ ] Deckle edges on meaning card feel natural, not machine-stamped
- [ ] Pouch drawstring pulls smoothly and holds closed
- [ ] Care card stays in lid pocket, doesn't fall out
- [ ] No adhesive smell, chemical odor, or off-gassing from any component
- [ ] All typography is legible and correctly spelled
- [ ] Overall experience from mailer to bracelet takes approximately 30 seconds
