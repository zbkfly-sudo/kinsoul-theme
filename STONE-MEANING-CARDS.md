## Production Notes

### Typography Specifications

**Front — Stone Name:**
- Font: Cormorant Garamond or similar elegant serif
- Weight: Regular or Light
- Size: 18–22pt
- Tracking: 0.3em (wide letter-spacing)
- Case: ALL CAPS

**Front — Poetic Line:**
- Font: Cormorant Garamond Italic
- Size: 10–11pt
- Tracking: normal
- Case: Sentence case
- Line height: 1.6

**Back — Body Text:**
- Font: Jost Light or similar geometric sans-serif
- Size: 7–8pt
- Tracking: 0.02em
- Case: Sentence case
- Line height: 1.5
- Color: `#4A4A4A` (warm charcoal, not pure black)

**Back — Brand Mark:**
- Font: Jost Regular
- Size: 6pt
- Tracking: 0.25em
- Case: ALL CAPS
- Position: Bottom center, with 8mm margin from edge

### Print Color
- Single color throughout: warm charcoal `#4A4A4A`
- Letterpress printing recommended for tactile impression
- If letterpress not feasible, use matte offset with no varnish

### Paper
- 350gsm uncoated cotton paper (e.g., Crane Lettra, Mohawk Superfine, or equivalent)
- Natural white / cream — NOT bright white
- Deckle edge on all four sides (hand-torn or deckle-cut)

### Quantity per Production Run
- Print 6 versions simultaneously to minimize setup costs
- Suggested ratio based on expected sales mix:
  - Aura: 10%
  - Terra: 15%
  - Ember: 20%
  - Obsidian: 20%
  - Serenity: 20%
  - Soleil: 15%
- Adjust after first quarter of sales data
