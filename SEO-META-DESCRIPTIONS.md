## Product Pages (example: Ember)
**Where to set:** Shopify Admin → Products → Ember → Edit SEO

**Current issue:** Meta description is 320 chars with non-breaking spaces, gets truncated poorly.

**Recommended title tag:**
```
Ember Bracelet — Red Agate & Pearl Silver Bar | Kinsoul Energy
```

**Recommended meta description (150 chars):**
```
Persian red agate and freshwater pearl silver bars, strung by hand on elastic cord. S925 sterling silver details. $228 — free shipping over $200.
```

**Note for other products — follow this pattern:**
- [Product Name] Bracelet — [Key Materials] | Kinsoul Energy
- Description: lead with the distinctive materials, mention handmade + silver, end with price and shipping.

## Shopify Admin Steps

### For Homepage title & description:
1. Go to **Online Store → Preferences**
2. Scroll to "Title and meta description"
3. Enter the title and description above
4. Save

### For each page/collection/product:
1. Go to the item in Admin
2. Scroll to bottom → click **"Edit website SEO"**
3. Enter the title and description
4. Save

### For Hero image alt text:
1. Go to **Online Store → Themes → Customize**
2. Click on the Hero section
3. Click on the image
4. In the image settings, add alt text: **"Close-up of a woman wearing a handmade Kinsoul Energy gemstone bracelet with natural pearls and red agate"**
5. Save
